About Sanjivani College

Our Vision

Sanjivani College aims to become a leading institution in higher education by fostering innovation, critical thinking, and ethical leadership among students. We are dedicated to excellence in education, research, and community service.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive and holistic education that empowers students to be global citizens. We aim to cultivate curiosity, creativity, and competence through state-of-the-art infrastructure and industry-oriented curriculum.

Why Choose Us

Sanjivani College offers a dynamic and inclusive environment, world-class faculty, hands-on learning opportunities, and a strong placement record. Our partnerships with top tech companies and research institutes give students a competitive edge.

Infrastructure & Facilities

We provide a modern campus with fully equipped labs, libraries, smart classrooms, auditoriums, sports complexes, and hostels. Our facilities are designed to enhance both academic and extracurricular growth.

Accreditations & Rankings

Sanjivani College is accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade and recognized by UGC and AICTE. We are consistently ranked among the top institutions in India for technical and professional education.

Leadership

Under the guidance of our experienced and visionary leadership team, Sanjivani College continues to break new ground in educational excellence. Our principal and deans work closely with faculty and students to drive innovation and quality.
