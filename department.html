Department of Computer Science

Overview

The Computer Science Department is committed to fostering excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. It offers a comprehensive curriculum covering all major areas of computing.

Programs Offered

We offer undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs tailored to equip students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills in modern computing technologies.

Faculty

Our department boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced professors, researchers, and industry professionals who are dedicated to mentoring and guiding students.

Research Areas

Our primary areas of research include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Data Science, Blockchain, and Cloud Computing.

Department Highlights

Modern laboratories, industry collaborations, technical fests, workshops, student clubs, and continuous assessment make our department a vibrant place to learn and grow.
